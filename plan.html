Project: App-Allergic-Prevention

Why and Background


My project is made to combat allergies in our ever more allergy-prone societies. This project is made with the idea of following the UN SDG goal, health and well-being, goal numbered three. Allergies both plague our health, with deadly allergy attacks, and pain and discontent, from decreasing quality of life. Overall, allergies hinder progress towards the SDG goal of health and well-being. Stopping or even decreasing allergies, however slightly, would greatly advance this SDG goal.

My Action


To act in aid of my SDG goal, "Health and Well-Being", I have decided to create my own implementation of an allergy tracking app. In recent years, my immune system and many bodily organs have been acting up, out of harmony. I have faced many allergy attacks, like hives, and many more I cannot pronounce. To solve these problems, or at least mitigate them, I have decided to make an app where I can log in. It should have an easy and fast journal of what I'm eating, when my stomach reacts, and a tracker where I can look up what was in my diet or the temperature and weather, which could have caused the event.

Example User Flow


  1. Login/Signup - Only if the user is not already logged in upon install. Requires Internal Storage or a server, depending on infrastructure and tech-stack.
  2. Dashboard - Recent items on top of the screen, some sort of "days since allergy incident or bodily malfunction" display. Two buttons sending the user to one of the two next elements.
  3. Buttons of Dashboard:
    • Report Diet Button - Makes a quick entry to the date with two columns: Food::String and Weather::Triple::Temp,Wind,Rain,Humidity
    • Report Bodily Malfunction - Adds an entry to the date with a column in the likely SQL-like database of the Report Diet Button.
